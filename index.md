# Privacy Policy Section

## Data Collection and Usage

### Information We Collect
- **Email Address:** Required for account creation and authentication
- **Journal Entries:** Your written reflections and spiritual thoughts
- **AI Processing Data:** Text submitted for prayer/verse generation
- **Theme Classifications:** Emotional/spiritual themes identified from your entries
- **Verse Selections:** Bible verses selected for your entries
- **Prayer Content:** Generated prayers based on your reflections

### How We Use Your Data
- **Service Provision:** Generate personalized prayers and verse recommendations
- **Personalization:** Improve content recommendations based on your spiritual themes
- **Safety:** Detect and prevent harmful or inappropriate content
- **Account Management:** Maintain your journal entries and preferences

### Data Storage
- **Location:** Supabase database (encrypted at rest)
- **Duration:** Retained until you delete your account
- **Linking:** All data is linked to your user account identity
- **Backup:** Secure backups maintained for data protection

### AI Processing
- **Input:** Your journal entries are processed by AI systems
- **Purpose:** Generate personalized prayers and identify spiritual themes
- **No Training:** Your data is not used to train external AI models
- **Vendors:** AI processing occurs through our internal systems

### Data Sharing
- **No Third-Party Sharing:** We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data
- **Service Providers:** Only share with essential service providers (Supabase)
- **Legal Requirements:** We may disclose data if required by law

### Your Rights
- **Access:** View all data we have about you
- **Correction:** Update or correct your personal information
- **Deletion:** Delete your account and all associated data
- **Portability:** Export your data in a readable format
- **Opt-Out:** Stop using the service to cease future data collection

### No Tracking
- **No Analytics:** We do not use Google Analytics or similar tracking
- **No Advertising:** We do not track you for advertising purposes
- **No Profiling:** We do not build profiles for third-party use
