# Privacy Policy

## Data Collection and Usage

### Information We Collect

- **Email Address:** Required for account creation, authentication, and account management.
- **Journal Entries:** Your written reflections and spiritual thoughts entered into the app.
- **AI Processing Data:** Text submitted for prayer generation, verse recommendations, or other AI-powered features.
- **Theme Classifications:** Emotional or spiritual themes identified from your entries to improve personalization.
- **Verse Selections:** Bible verses selected or associated with your entries.
- **Prayer Content:** Generated prayers based on your reflections.
- **Moderation and Safety Signals:** Information used to detect harmful, abusive, or inappropriate content and maintain a safe environment.

---

### How We Use Your Data

- **Service Provision:** Generate personalized prayers, insights, and verse recommendations.
- **Personalization:** Improve content recommendations based on your spiritual themes and usage.
- **Safety:** Detect and prevent harmful or inappropriate content and enforce community guidelines.
- **Account Management:** Maintain your account, saved entries, and preferences.

---

### Data Storage

- **Location:** Data is stored using secure cloud infrastructure provided by Supabase.
- **Security:** Data is encrypted in transit and at rest using industry-standard security practices.
- **Duration:** Data is retained until you delete your account or request deletion.
- **Linking:** Data is associated with your user account identity to provide app functionality.
- **Backup:** Secure backups may be maintained for reliability and recovery purposes.

---

### AI Processing

- **Input:** Your journal entries and submitted text may be processed by artificial intelligence systems.
- **Purpose:** Generate personalized prayers, insights, and identify spiritual themes.
- **Third-Party Providers:** AI processing may involve trusted third-party service providers acting on our behalf.
- **No Training:** Your personal data is not used to train publicly available AI models.
- **User Control:** You may choose not to use AI features by not submitting content for processing.

---

### Data Sharing

- **No Sale of Data:** We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.
- **Service Providers:** We share data only with essential providers necessary to operate the app, such as:
  - Supabase (authentication and database infrastructure)
  - AI processing providers acting as data processors
- **Legal Requirements:** We may disclose information if required by law or to protect rights, safety, or security.

---

### Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access:** View the information associated with your account.
- **Correction:** Update or correct inaccurate personal information.
- **Deletion:** Delete your account and associated data.
- **Portability:** Export your data in a readable format.
- **Withdrawal:** Stop using the service to cease future data collection.

Requests can be made through the app or by contacting support.

---

### No Tracking

- **No Cross-App Tracking:** We do not track users across third-party apps or websites.
- **No Advertising:** We do not use your data for advertising or marketing profiling.
- **No Third-Party Analytics for Advertising:** We do not share data with advertisers or data brokers.

---

### Children’s Privacy

This app is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

---

### Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted within the app and/or on our website.
